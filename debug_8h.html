Enumerator
lvl_unset 

Internal use only, do not use for logging.

lvl_error 

Error: something did not work.

lvl_warning 

Warning: something may not have worked.

lvl_info 

Informational message. Should make sense to non-programmers.

lvl_debug 

Debug output: (almost) anything goes.
