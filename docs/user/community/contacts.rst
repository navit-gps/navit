.. _contact:

Contacts
========

You can contact Navit :ref:`team` and frequent users for help or
advice through a variety of methods. Though you can use any of the
methods below, Discord will probably result the quickest response.

.. _discord:

Getting in Touch
================

We are on Discord. In addition to the text-chats we also have some Broadcast
Channels where we share relevant information like new releases.


Feel free to join: https://discord.gg/ADfXxZx
